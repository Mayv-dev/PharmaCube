User Manual For the ORO

    Getting Started
  • Make sure the ORO is on a level surface
  • Plug both plugs into wall sockets.
  • A green light should flash, then hold steady to indicate it is connected to the internet
    Filling the ORO
  • Your schedule should be automatically synched with the ORO as long as it has an internet connection
  • Press the "fill organiser" button
  • The lid should now rotate to a compartment that is used in your schedule
  • Place the correct medication in that compartment. Consult the app if you are unsure
  • Press the button again to move on to the next compartment
  • Repeat this process until you arrive at the starting state
    Taking your Medication
  • When it is time to take medication, the ORO will play a noise three times
  • Press the "take medication" button
  • Take your medication
  • Press the button again to close the lid
    Troubleshooting

  • The ORO's LED is green, but still has no internet

    Make sure the access point has internet as the light only shows that the ORO is connected to it.
